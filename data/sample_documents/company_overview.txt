# Acme Corporation - Company Overview

Acme Corporation is a leading technology company founded in 2010 by Alice Johnson. The company specializes in enterprise software solutions and has grown to serve over 10,000 clients worldwide.

## Mission Statement

"To empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency."

## Company Values

- **Innovation**: We constantly push boundaries and explore new technologies
- **Integrity**: We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations  
- **Collaboration**: We believe in the power of teamwork and open communication
- **Excellence**: We strive for excellence in everything we do
- **Customer Focus**: Our customers' success is our success

## Company History

### 2010: The Beginning
Alice Johnson founded Acme Corp with a vision to revolutionize enterprise software. Starting with just 5 employees in a small office, the company launched its first product - a cloud-based project management tool.

### 2015: Major Growth
The company reached 100 employees and secured Series B funding of $50 million. This allowed expansion into new markets and the development of additional product lines.

### 2020: Going Global
Acme Corp opened international offices in London, Tokyo, and Singapore. The employee count reached 500, and annual revenue exceeded $100 million.

### 2024: AI Innovation
Under the leadership of CTO Bob Smith, Acme launched Project Phoenix - an ambitious initiative to integrate AI across all product offerings.

## Current Status

- **Employees**: 500+
- **Annual Revenue**: $150 million
- **Clients**: 10,000+
- **Offices**: 4 (San Francisco, London, Tokyo, Singapore)
- **Products**: 12 major product lines
