# Acme Corporation - Employee Benefits Guide

## Overview

Acme Corporation is committed to providing comprehensive benefits that support our employees' health, financial security, and work-life balance.

## Health & Wellness

### Medical Insurance
- **Provider**: BlueCross BlueShield
- **Coverage**: Medical, dental, vision
- **Employee Cost**: Company pays 90% of premiums
- **Family Coverage**: Available at subsidized rates
- **Enrollment**: Benefits start on first day of employment

### Dental Insurance
- **Provider**: Delta Dental
- **Coverage**: Preventive, basic, and major services
- **Annual Maximum**: $2,000 per person
- **Employee Cost**: 100% company paid

### Vision Insurance
- **Provider**: VSP
- **Coverage**: Eye exams, lenses, frames
- **Frequency**: Annual eye exam, new lenses/frames every 2 years
- **Employee Cost**: 100% company paid

### Mental Health
- **Counseling Services**: 12 free sessions per year through Employee Assistance Program
- **Provider Network**: Extensive network of licensed therapists
- **Confidentiality**: 100% confidential
- **Access**: 24/7 crisis hotline available

### Wellness Programs
- **Gym Membership**: $50/month reimbursement
- **Wellness Challenges**: Quarterly fitness challenges with prizes
- **Healthy Snacks**: Free fruits and healthy snacks in all offices
- **Standing Desks**: Available upon request
- **Ergonomic Equipment**: Fully covered

## Time Off

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **Accrual Rate**: 1.25 days per month (15 days per year)
- **Year 3+**: 1.67 days per month (20 days per year)
- **Year 5+**: 2.08 days per month (25 days per year)
- **Carryover**: Up to 5 days can roll over to next year
- **Usage**: Must be submitted 2 weeks in advance for trips > 5 days

### Holidays
- **Paid Holidays**: 12 per year
  - New Year's Day
  - Martin Luther King Jr. Day
  - President's Day
  - Memorial Day
  - Independence Day
  - Labor Day
  - Thanksgiving (2 days)
  - Christmas (2 days)
  - 2 Floating Holidays (your choice)

### Sick Leave
- **Accrual**: 1 day per month (12 days per year)
- **No Carryover**: Use it or lose it
- **Documentation**: Doctor's note required for 3+ consecutive days
- **Family Care**: Can be used for sick family members

### Parental Leave
- **Maternity Leave**: 16 weeks fully paid
- **Paternity Leave**: 8 weeks fully paid
- **Adoption Leave**: 12 weeks fully paid
- **Flexibility**: Can be taken within first year
- **Job Protection**: Guaranteed return to same or equivalent role

## Financial Benefits

### Retirement

#### 401(k) Plan
- **Provider**: Fidelity Investments
- **Eligibility**: Immediate (day 1)
- **Employee Contribution**: Up to IRS maximum
- **Company Match**: 100% match up to 6% of salary
- **Vesting**: Immediate (100% vested from day 1)
- **Investment Options**: 25+ fund options

#### Stock Options
- **Eligibility**: All employees
- **Grant**: Based on level and performance
- **Vesting Schedule**: 4 years (25% per year)
- **Strike Price**: Fair market value at grant date

### Bonuses
- **Annual Bonus**: 10-20% of base salary (performance-based)
- **Spot Bonuses**: $500-$2,000 for exceptional work
- **Referral Bonus**: $5,000 for successful referrals

### Professional Development
- **Annual Budget**: $2,000 per employee
- **Uses**: Conferences, courses, certifications, books
- **Conference Travel**: Fully covered (separate from $2,000 budget)
- **Internal Training**: Unlimited free access to LinkedIn Learning

## Work-Life Balance

### Flexible Work
- **Remote Work**: 3 days per week work from home
- **Core Hours**: 10 AM - 3 PM (must be available)
- **Flexible Schedule**: Start between 7-10 AM
- **Equipment**: Company provides laptop, monitor, accessories

### Commuter Benefits
- **Transit Pass**: Up to $280/month pre-tax
- **Parking**: $150/month subsidy
- **Bike Incentive**: $50/month for bike commuters

### Family Support
- **Childcare Subsidy**: $500/month
- **Backup Childcare**: 10 days per year through Bright Horizons
- **Elder Care**: Support and resources through EAP

## Life & Disability Insurance

### Life Insurance
- **Basic**: 2x annual salary (company paid)
- **Supplemental**: Can purchase up to 5x salary
- **Dependent Coverage**: Available for spouse and children

### Disability Insurance
- **Short-term**: 60% of salary for up to 6 months
- **Long-term**: 60% of salary after 6 months
- **Coverage**: Covers illness and injury
