## Cross-Functional Collaboration

Teams frequently collaborate on major initiatives:

- **Project Phoenix**: ML Team + Backend Team + Frontend Team
- **Customer Portal Redesign**: Frontend Team + UX Team + Marketing
- **Infrastructure Modernization**: DevOps Team + Backend Team
- **Security Initiative**: All engineering teams + External consultants
